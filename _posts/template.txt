hello. here is the boilerplate for my posts, just copy and paste into a .yml file inside the _posts folder and change the date, the title, and pretty much everything else to fit the post
post creation docs: https://chirpy.cotes.page/posts/write-a-new-post/


---
title: TITLE
date: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS -0500
categories: [TOP_CATEGORIE, SUB_CATEGORIE]
tags: [TAG]     # TAG names should always be lowercase
author: <author_id>
comments: true
toc: true
---
